Dr. Uma Paleti – A Visionary in Dermatology & Aesthetic Medicine

From a young age, Dr. Uma Paleti was captivated by the intricate connection between skin health and overall well-being. Her journey into medicine began with an unwavering desire to help people regain their confidence and improve their quality of life. Over the years, this passion evolved into a focused mission: to master the art and science of dermatology and cosmetology.

With a deep academic foundation and years of clinical experience, Dr. Uma Paleti has become a trusted expert in both medical and aesthetic dermatology. Her approach is rooted in empathy, precision, and innovation. She stays at the forefront of global advancements, regularly attending international workshops, conferences, and training programs to bring the best, evidence-based treatments to her patients in India.

What sets Dr. Uma apart is her holistic understanding of each patient’s unique skin profile, lifestyle, and goals. Whether it’s treating complex dermatological conditions, offering personalized anti-aging therapies, or enhancing beauty through non-surgical aesthetic solutions — she blends medical expertise with artistic finesse. Her gentle demeanor and honest guidance have made her a favorite among patients of all ages.

At Ananya Skin & Laser Clinic, she has built more than just a space for treatment — she has created a haven of care, compassion, and transformation. Her commitment to using only FDA-approved, globally trusted technologies ensures that each treatment is not only effective but also safe and comfortable.

For Dr. Uma Paleti, dermatology is not merely a profession — it is a lifelong passion and purpose. Her dream is to empower individuals to embrace their natural beauty with confidence, grace, and radiant health.
